Our Culture

Named one of the “Best Places to Work” by Austin Business Journal, Umbel employees are passionate, self-disciplined and multi-talented individuals who are passionate about their work both inside and outside the office. Umbel offers an upbeat work environment and exceptional benefits with offices in the historic Seaholm power plant of downtown Austin, Texas.

Love to travel? So do we. We offer employees unlimited vacation so they can relax, unwind and explore the world. We even require mandatory vacation periods for all employees. Non-stop learning is in our fiber. From industry conferences to inspirational leaders, we encourage our employees to continuously further their knowledge.Nothing like a little healthy competition, right? We offer fitness rewards, discounted gym membership and a new Fitbit for every employee.We feel lucky to be living in Austin and show our gratitude by giving back to our community. We support organizations our employees are most passionate about with donation matching, company events and more. If having a front desk made out of La Croix boxes isn’t evidence enough, our snack bar is brimming with healthy goodies, the finest coffee, and all-you-can-drink La Croix.

About Us

We believe data should be the most valuable asset that a company owns. To that end, Umbel specializes in helping some of the most recognizable names in sports, media, entertainment, and the nonprofit sector aggregate customer data, gather insights and drive revenue. We are passionate about making data beautiful and actionable so you are empowered to improve your business, improve customer experiences and improve the world.
